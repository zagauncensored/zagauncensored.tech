About Zaga Uncensored

Zaga Uncensored is a movement powered by the students of our school, aiming to create a positive change in our educational environment. Our goal is to ensure that every student's voice is heard, and to work together to address the issues that affect our education and well-being.

Our Objectives

Defeat censorship

Allowing students to speak freely without fear of unjust punishment that may violate their fundamental freedom of expression.

Introduce transparency

Encouraging faculty to be more transparent about data safety, mark calculations, school operations and more.

Establish democracy

Getting students involved in decisions regarding their own education and giving them the power to make those decisions.

Join the Movement

If you are passionate about creating a better future for our school, we invite you to join Zaga Uncensored. Together, we can make a difference and create lasting change for the betterment of our school and community.
